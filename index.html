Who I am

A life built on feeling.
Always becoming.

I'm Moa. Yoga teacher, space holder, writer, and co-founder of House of Soma.

From a young age movement showed me something. A sense of freedom and connection that has guided me ever since. What began as a physical yoga practice has become my way of life.

Over ten years my curiosity has led me across styles and continents. From yin and creative vinyasa to somatic movement and embodied expression. From Stockholm to Spain, India to Nicaragua. Always moving. Always feeling.

I teach with presence, warmth, and creativity and from lived experience. Not to perfect the form but to move beneath it. Into feeling, into the body's quiet wisdom, into what's already true and alive in you.

I'm also trained in sound healing and Reiki, and I hold space for writing and journaling as embodied practice.

With me, the invitation is simple. To move out of the head and into the body. To soften. To trust. To experience joy not as a destination but as a living, breathing act of resilience.

Retreat

House of Soma

Retreats for women who want to be seen, explore, and connect. Feel held in a community that sees you. Reconnect with your creativity. Remember your power.

You're not too much, too sensitive, or too late. You're right on time. Inside our retreats, we move beyond overthinking and into the body. You'll walk away with tools to trust yourself again, build deep connections, and live life on your terms.

House of Soma didn't start as a business idea. It started as a friendship. A friendship that showed us what's possible when women meet each other with openness, trust, and joy. This is the space we're devoted to creating.
